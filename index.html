Our Team

Chef de Cuisine

Matt Abé

Matt Abé has spent the past ten years rising through the ranks of the Gordon Ramsay Group in London, where he now runs the kitchen as Chef de Cuisine at the three Michelin-starred Restaurant Gordon Ramsay.

Restaurant Manager

Enrico Baronetto

Enrico Baronetto, Restaurant Manager at three Michelin-starred Restaurant Gordon Ramsay brings a wealth of international experience from Europe’s finest establishments to the heart of London.

Maitre d'

Jean-Claude Breton

Maitre d' Jean-Claude Breton initially met Gordon in France and joined him for the opening of Aubergine in London in 1993. Their relationship grew over the years and he is now absorbed in ensuring the utmost care and service for guests dining at Restaurant Gordon Ramsay.

Consultant Chef

Clare Smyth MBE

Born in Northern Ireland, Clare moved to England at 16 to learn the skills which would eventually make her a world class chef. On completing her studies Clare’s thirst for knowledge drove her to stage under many influential chefs around the world.
